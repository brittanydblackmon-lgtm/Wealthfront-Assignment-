Why Wealthfront

Chime helps people stabilize. Wealthfront helps them grow. Same mission, next chapter. I want to build the programs that move people from surviving to thriving.

The Problem We Were Solving

Chime's referral program was growing but direct deposit conversion wasn't keeping pace

North Star Metric

30-Day Direct Deposit Conversion

When a referred member sets up direct deposit within 30 days, Chime becomes their primary bank. Everything downstream compounds from this moment.

The Gap

Generic Offers Weren't Moving Behavior

Hundreds of thousands of referred members enrolled but didn't reach the DD milestone. A flat $100 offer to everyone was leaving growth on the table.

Business Goal

Increase 30D DD CVR across referred member base while maintaining positive CAC

Key Constraint

Standard LTOs cost $25-$400 per acquisition. Needed conversion lift without proportionally increasing spend

My Role

Owned lifecycle strategy across both C1 (existing Chime members who refer) and C2 (newly referred members) journeys. Urgency Offer targeted C1 only, while Mystery Bonus + C2 Nudge activated C2s. Apple Pay was a separate full-base GTM for iOS 16.2+ members focused on IIT funding, not referrals.

VEGA: Variable Evergreen General Audience

From failed ML model to personalized offer engine scaling to 23.5M members

1

The Hypothesis

Members convert better when the offer reflects their predicted likelihood, not a flat $100 for everyone.

2

First Attempt: DSML Optimization

ML-powered targeting yielded neutral lift after adjusting for sample imbalance. Diagnosed the failure and pivoted fast.

3

The Pivot: Randomized Offer Testing

Shifted to randomized offer amounts ($100, $125, $150, $200) across 7M+ members. Proper holdout via ExPlat. 50/50 split. 11-week runtime.

4

Result: $125 Variant Won. Scaled to GA.

+18% lift in 30D DDers. All variants positive ROI. Rolled to 23.5M active members. ~20K newly enrolled members targeted per day ongoing.

What I Learned and How I'd Apply It

From Chime's referral and funding lifecycle to Wealthfront's lending expansion

Key Learnings

Personalization beats volume. VEGA proved the right offer to the right person outperforms blasting everyone, even when the ML model fails on the first pass.

Measure what you cause. Holdout groups are non-negotiable. Incremental lift is the only number that matters for budget decisions.

Multichannel means meeting people where they are. Apple Pay's success came from 5 channels each doing a different job in the journey.

How I'd Think About Wealthfront Lending
01

Signal-Triggered Lending Cross-Sell

Trigger when members show high-intent signals: meaningful investable assets, lending page browse, and a lending-intent goal in Path. Personalize message, rate framing, and CTA by each member's actual financial profile.

02

Bundle Experiment With Holdouts

Run a controlled test of Cash Account + Lending versus Investing + Lending entry narratives. Use holdouts and incremental lift readouts (VEGA methodology) to determine which bundle truly increases funded lending applications.

03

Expansion-State Personalization

In newly launched states, prioritize existing members as warm leads and personalize offers by current product usage before scaling to cold acquisition. Measure geo-level incrementality before broad budget expansion.

The through line from Chime to Wealthfront: bring personalization + experimentation discipline to lending so each canvas is behavior-triggered, holdout-measured, and scaled only when incremental lift is proven.

How I Measure What Matters

I measure what I cause, not just what happens: platform reporting, internal validation, then holdout-validated incrementality.

1) Platform Reported

Topline ESP/platform conversions show directional movement but can over-credit impact.

3) Holdout-Validated Incremental

Only the lift versus a proper holdout answers, “Did this program actually cause behavior change?”

This is the number used for budget and rollout decisions.
