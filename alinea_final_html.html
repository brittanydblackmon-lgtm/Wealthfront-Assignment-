VEGA Applied to Referrals

At Chime: VEGA dynamically populated variable incentive amounts across referral comms. Proven in promos, then scaled to evergreen across 23.5M members.

At Alinea: same framework tests variable referral incentives credited to the investment account.

Randomize first, collect clean data, then let the model optimize spend where it actually changes behavior.

Churn Propensity Model

At Chime: predictive modeling identified members most likely to set up direct deposit.

At Alinea: same methodology identifies members most likely to cancel before renewal. Signals: app open frequency, investment inactivity, Allie conversation drop-off.

Proactive comms fire before the member consciously decides to leave.

Proactive Trust Infrastructure + Trustpilot API

The problem: Trustpilot complaints are a lifecycle problem. Members feel blindsided by withdrawals and fees because nobody set expectations.

The Trustpilot API: At Chime, built Trustpilot's Create Invitation API into Braze email CTAs. Generates a unique member-specific review URL at send time. No member data shared with Trustpilot unless they actually leave a review.

Why it works: Directs member to the review flow, not the landing page. At Chime: 3.8 targeting 4+. At Alinea: same approach to raise 2.6 toward 4+.

Withdrawal Anxiety Sequence: Multi-step Money Map triggered the moment a withdrawal is initiated. Day 1: funds being settled. Day 2: funds on the way with expected date. Eliminates the number one support ticket type and the number one driver of 1-star reviews.

In-App Inbox and Notifications

At Chime: inbox notifications gave members a persistent message center inside the app.

At Alinea: members revisit Allie conversations and receive account updates without leaving the product.

In-app banners for very important moments — pop up on app open, dismissible. Same urgency as SMS but inside the product where they already are.

Work Sample: VEGA and the Urgency Offer

How dynamic offer personalization works at Chime — and how the same framework applies at Alinea.

What VEGA Is

VEGA is a dynamic offer attribution framework. It populates a personalized incentive amount into any comm across any channel.

First attempt: ML model gave the highest offers to members already most likely to refer. The spend was backwards.

The pivot: Moved to randomized bucketing. 25% of members each received $100, $125, $150, or $200. Clean data, no prediction bias.

The result: $125 drove +18% lift in 30-day direct deposit conversions. All variants positive ROI. Scaled to 23.5M members.

What's next: A T-learner uplift model now assigns the right offer per member automatically.

How This Applies at Alinea

Apply randomized bucketing to referral incentives credited to the investment account.

Collect clean behavioral data on who refers, when, and for how much. Let the data build the Predictive Intelligence Layer.

Same principle for churn: randomize retention offers, find the threshold that changes behavior, then optimize with a model.

Randomize first. Prove it works. Then scale it.

How I Know It's Actually Working

From pre-launch validation to holdout-proven incrementality. Built to work even without a dedicated analyst.

Pre-Launch — Before the First Send
Step 1: Audience Sizing

Confirm eligible audience is large enough to reach statistical significance. If no analyst, use CRM platform counts cross-referenced against event trigger data.

Step 2: Event Tracking Confirmed

Confirm events and attributes are firing correctly. Canvas entries in CRM must match the expected cohort size before anything launches.

Step 3: Holdout Established

10% of eligible members withheld before first send. Without this baseline no conversion number can be trusted. Non-negotiable.

During Launch — Checkpoints
24 Hours

Canvas entries match expected cohort? Messages sending? If numbers are off, pause before it runs further.

3 Days

Open rates, click rates, delivery rate. Members progressing through canvas steps?

7 Days

Exits and conversions starting to show. Which touchpoints are driving action?

30 Days

Full measurement window. CRM data cross-referenced against backend. Holdout comparison run.

Post-Launch — The Three Numbers That Matter
X: Platform Reported

Opens, clicks, sends, canvas entries. What the CRM says. Never used alone for decisions. Always overstates impact.

Y: Internally Validated

Cross-reference CRM data against backend conversion data. Removes false positives. If no analyst, use strict attribution windows in the CRM platform.

Z: Holdout-Validated Incremental

The only number used for budget decisions. What did lifecycle actually cause versus what would have happened organically? This is how VEGA's +18% was confirmed real.
